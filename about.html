Who We Are

Triple E (EEE) Business Company was established in 1996 as a Construction, Agriculture and trading company, amalgamating the knowledge and experiences of its founding partners, which span over 35 years Of, Logistic, research, development and management work, financial and Other architectural related services.

Triple E (EEE) Business Company provides business development services, through a wide range of technical activities, in support of private sector development. The focus is primarily on the development of small and medium scale enterprises as a direct service provider, as well as in partnership with government and non-Governmental, donors, investors, financial institutions and international development organizations.

Triple E (EEE) Business Company services are of high technical quality thanks to the qualifications of a strong team of multidisciplinary Engineers with backgrounds in Logistics and Construction Of all kinds, socio-economic development, business management and Agro-business.

TO be a highly ethical and quality service provider to a vibrant private sector in the Mano River Union States of Sierra Leone, Guinea and Liberia.

TO provide a range of high-quality technical assistance and professional services essential for the development and implementation of viable business Company and development projects in a professional, timely and cost-effective manner.

• To provide services in the form of construction of public structures / buildings so as to meet the growing demand of the market;
• TO also provide services in the form of rehabilitation of dilapidated public and private structures/ buildings to meet the growing demand of the market;
• To offer services in the area of Logistics (clearing and forwarding)
• To construct and rehabilitate bridges / culverts and roads;
• Maximize profits from the services (Supplies, Construction, rehabilitation and training services offered to clients;
• The creation of marketing linkages through the establishment of networks Local builders / Contractors, suppliers, services providers and Agricultural Business Enterprises;
• TO assist small and medium scale Enterprises (SMEs) to develop a commercial rural private network of agro-dealers to supply the growing demand of inputs in collaboration With MAFFS and Other Agribusiness institutions;
• Build/strengthen key private sector Companies and associations, including agro-dealers to provide other services
